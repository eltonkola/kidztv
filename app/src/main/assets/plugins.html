How to add videos

You can rip your dvds and/or copy your video files on your device, with an usb cable, or other software under /sdcard.kidztv., or you can download the free videos with the build in plugin. But you can also use third party plugins, but for that you will need to download and install them separately.

Download plugins

A list of compatible plugins can be found on our github page: https://github.com/eltonkola/kidztv .
Please follow the instruction from there, on how to install and use other plugins (like the youtube downloader plugin).

Develop plugins

Making a new plugin is very easy and simple, for more info, read the wiki page on github: https://github.com/eltonkola/kidztv .
